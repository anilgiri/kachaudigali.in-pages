About Kachaudi Gali

Kachaudi Gali is more than just a food blog - it’s a celebration of India’s rich street food culture. Our mission is to:

  1. Document and preserve traditional street food recipes
  2. Share authentic food experiences from across India
  3. Connect food lovers with the best street food spots
  4. Promote the diverse culinary heritage of Indian street food

Our Story

Founded by food enthusiasts who believe in the power of street food to bring people together, Kachaudi Gali aims to be your trusted companion in exploring the vibrant world of Indian street cuisine.
